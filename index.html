Our All-Inclusive Process

Our All-Inclusive ProcessOur All-Inclusive ProcessOur All-Inclusive Process

Reserch

Research is the most crucial part of any project. One can never find a solution until he has a clear idea of what he’s actually working on. We start by researching and gathering comprehensive data about your project and formulate all possible solutions, opportunities for improvements and positive outcomes.

IDEATION

After the basic research & requirements, we share the collected and gathered data with you to give you inspiration and ideas to get to know your preference. Our team comes together to brainstorm and compare ideas to decide on the best features for your project.

DESIGN

In this step we design your concepts by creating multiple small-scale prototypes that resemble the finished product. Throughout this process, we continuously look for new areas of improvement as well as user experience validation of existing concepts.

REVISIONS

Once the designing step is verified and the client chooses one concept, it is time to revise the design from start so it meets the goals and outlines which involves the refinement, finding issues and fixing, and improvement of solution and it continues throughout the design process, often even after a project is delivered to customers.

DEVELOPMENT

We take preliminary ideas and develop multiple design solutions, keeping client’s requirements in our minds.

FINALIZATION

We then present the final product to as many people as possible to take insightful comments and considering those, we then reflect and decide if or to what extent it should be incorporated before delivering. When everything is well adjusted, we deliver final files to our valuable client.
